Additional Coverage Options:

  • Accelerated Death Benefit Rider

    This rider allows policyholders to access a portion of their death benefit if they are diagnosed with a terminal illness.

  • Child Protection Rider

    This rider provides life insurance coverage for the policyholder's children. In the unfortunate event of a child's death.

  • Accidental Death Benefit Rider

    In the event of death due to an accident, this rider provides an additional death benefit on top of the base policy coverage.

  • Long-Term Care Rider

    This rider provides coverage for long-term care expenses if the policyholder requires assistance with activities of daily living due to illness or injury.

  • Waiver of Premium Rider

    With the waiver of premium rider, your life insurance premiums are waived if you become totally disabled and unable to work.

  • Critical Illness Rider

    With this rider, policyholders receive a lump-sum payment if diagnosed with a covered critical illness such as cancer, heart attack, or stroke.
